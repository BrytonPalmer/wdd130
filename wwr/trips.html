Rapids Rafting Trips

Our Trips

At Rapids Rafting, we offer a variety of thrilling rafting trips designed to suit all levels of adventure seekers. From gentle floats perfect for families to adrenaline-pumping Class V rapids, our trips are crafted to provide unforgettable experiences. Join us as we explore breathtaking landscapes, navigate wild waters, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Featured Trips

Westwater Canyon

  • Location: Colorado River, Utah
  • Duration: 1 day - 17 miles
  • Rapids difficulty: Class III to IV
  • Best time to go: May to September
  • Highlights: Red rock formations, wildlife, hiking

Westwater Canyon offers thrilling rapids and stunning scenery, including the famous “Westwater Falls.”

Cataract Canyon

  • Location: Southeastern Utah, along the Colorado River
  • Duration: 3-4 days - 100 miles from Moab to Lake Powell
  • Rapids difficulty: Class III to Class V
  • Best time to go: May to September
  • Highlights: Red rock formations, wildlife, hiking

Cataract Canyon is known for its challenging rapids and breathtaking landscapes.

Desolation Canyon

  • Location: Eastern Utah, along the Green River
  • Duration: 5-6 days - 84-88 miles from Sand Wash to Swasey's Boat Ramp
  • Rapids difficulty: Class II to Class III
  • Best time to go: May to September
  • Highlights: Scenic views, wildlife, hiking

Desolation Canyon is perfect for families and beginners, offering a serene yet adventurous experience.

Lodore Canyon

  • Location: Colorado and Utah border, along the Green River
  • Duration: 4-5 days - 44 miles from Split Mountain to Echo Park
  • Rapids difficulty: Class III to Class IV
  • Best time to go: May to September
  • Highlights: Dramatic canyon walls, wildlife, hiking

Lodore Canyon offers a mix of thrilling rapids and breathtaking scenery, including the famous “Gates of Lodore.”
