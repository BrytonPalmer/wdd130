Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States known for its diverse geography, including mountains, deserts, and national parks. It is home to five national parks, often referred to as the "Mighty 5": Zion, Bryce Canyon, Arches, Canyonlands, and Capitol Reef. The state is also known for its rich history, particularly related to the Mormon pioneers who settled there in the mid-1800s.
